About WestWatt

Founded in 2020, WestWatt Solution Ltd began with a mission to bring sustainable energy to every corner of Kenya. We now power schools, clinics, and businesses across East Africa.

Vision: To lead the transition to a sustainable future by making solar energy the most accessible and reliable power source for every home and business.
